Bharathi Park

Feel the slow pace of Pondicherry's life as you stroll in the Bharati Park, situated in the heart of Puducherry. The park with its shady trees and soothing green grass is the ideal spot to beat the heat.

Shri Aurobindo Ashram

The Sri Aurobindo Ashram, located on Rue de la Marine was founded by Sri Aurobindo and the Mother in 1926 for those who sought the spiritual life and were drawn by their philosophy.Most peaceful place.

Read More

Vysial Street

Declared as a UNESCO Heritage site in 2008, Vysial Street is one of the rare locations in Pondicherry where still old houses with the earliest examples of fusion between European and Tamil architecture can be seen.
